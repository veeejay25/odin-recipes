Orange Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken Dredge

  • 2 pcs chopped Onions
  • 6 cloves chopped Garlic
  • 1 kilo Pork belly chopped into cubes
  • 1/2 tsp Salt and Black pepper
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 2 cups Sprite
  • add Water as needed to soften the meat
  • 2 pcs dried bay leaves
  • 3 tbsp Vinegar
  • 1/3 tsp whole Peppercorn

    Instructions

  1. Saute chopped onions on high flame seting for about 20 seconds.
  2. Add chopped garlic and continue to saute for another 10 seconds.
  3. Add pork belly and continue to saute and let the fat render.
  4. Add ground black pepper and continue to render the fat.
  5. Add the soy sauce and oyster sauce, continue to saute until the sauce is reduced.
  6. Once reduced add the sprite, dried bay leaves, and whole peppercorn and slow cook for 1 hour
  7. After slow cooking set to high heat and saute for 5 minutes
  8. Add vinegar and slow cook for about 5 minutes.
  9. You could serve this with still a bit of sauce or continue to reduce the sauce to make the flavor more concentrated.
