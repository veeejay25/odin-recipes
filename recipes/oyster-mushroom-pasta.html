Oyster Mushroom Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Oyster mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups Fettuccine or preferred pasta
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saute for a minute.
  3. Add oyster mushrooms and cook until they release their juices and become tender.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and let simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Stir in the grated parmesan until a smooth sauce forms.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.
